About Sharda Setu

Sharda Setu is a revolutionary live learning platform designed specifically for low bandwidth areas. We're bridging the digital divide in education by making quality learning accessible to everyone, anywhere, anytime.

The Story Behind Sharda Setu

The idea for Sharda Setu was born from a simple observation: millions of students in rural and remote areas have the desire to learn but lack access to quality educational resources due to poor internet connectivity.


Our team of educators, engineers, and social impact enthusiasts came together with a shared vision: to create a learning platform that could work effectively even with limited bandwidth, bringing world-class education to every corner of the globe.


Named after the Sanskrit words "Sharda" (knowledge/learning) and "Setu" (bridge), our platform literally serves as a bridge to knowledge, connecting learners with educational opportunities regardless of their physical location or technological constraints.
